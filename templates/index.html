Dedicated Legal Professionals

June Wu

Barrister & Solicitor in Ontario, Canada

  • Holds a Master of Laws (LL.M.) from Osgoode Hall Law School at York University in Ontario, a Master of Laws from East China University of Political Science and Law in China, and a Bachelor's degree in English Literature from Wuhan University in China.
  • Brings a distinctive combination of legal, financial, and cross-border experience to her practice. Before practising law in Ontario, she worked at Bank of Montreal for over seven years, where she developed extensive knowledge of financial products, wealth management structures, estate planning strategies, and trust-related solutions.
  • Integrates her background in financial services with her legal training to provide strategic, practical, and forward-looking advice in wills, estate planning, and trusts. She leverages her experience in financial investments and estate structuring to help clients plan proactively, minimize tax exposure, manage risk, and protect what matters most to their families.
  • Based in Oakville, Ontario, June is committed to delivering high-quality and affordable legal services to her local community. She is fluent in Mandarin and dedicated to serving the Chinese community with culturally informed, clear, and accessible legal support.

Yanguo Yuan

Barrister & Solicitor in Ontario, Canada

  • Dr. Yuan holds a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Corporate Laws and a Professional Master of Laws (LL.M.) in Canadian Common Law from Osgoode Hall Law School. His academic training in corporate law provides a strong theoretical foundation that informs his practical, solutions-oriented approach to advising clients.
  • Dr. Yuan focuses his practice on corporate and commercial law, advising entrepreneurs, private companies, and investors on incorporations, corporate structuring and reorganizations, shareholders' agreements, governance matters, commercial contracts, and private mergers and acquisitions.
