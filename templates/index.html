Wills, Estates & Trusts

We provide comprehensive legal services in wills, estates, and trusts, assisting individuals and families at every stage of the planning and administration process. Our services include the preparation of wills and powers of attorney, the creation of trust arrangements, probate applications, estate administration, and estate-related dispute resolution.

We work closely with our clients to develop tailored estate plans that reflect their personal, financial, and family circumstances. Where appropriate, we also coordinate with professional trust companies and other advisors to ensure effective and seamless administration.

Our approach is practical, detail-oriented, and client-focused. We are committed to delivering clear legal advice and thoughtful planning strategies, enabling our clients to make informed decisions with confidence and peace of mind. From initial planning to final administration, we guide our clients through each step with care and professionalism.

Tax Legal Services

We provide practical and cost-effective legal services in tax matters, assisting individuals, businesses, and professionals in navigating their obligations and resolving disputes with the Canada Revenue Agency.

Our services include advising on and responding to audits and reassessments, preparing and filing Notices of Objection, and representing clients in tax disputes, including matters before the Tax Court of Canada. We have experience assisting clients with net worth audits, SR&ED-related disputes, and tax residency issues, as well as other tax compliance and advisory matters.

We understand that tax disputes can be complex and stressful. Our approach is practical, responsive, and solutions-oriented. We work closely with our clients to assess their situation, develop effective strategies, and communicate clearly with the CRA at each stage of the process.
