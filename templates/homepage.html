What We Do

Real Estate

We represent clients in residential and recreational real estate transactions throughout Muskoka, including cottage properties. Muskoka real estate often has a unique character, and our practice reflects an understanding of the local issues that can arise in waterfront and rural property transactions. Our work includes purchases, sales, refinancing, title transfers, and related matters, with a focus on careful preparation, practical advice, and keeping transactions organized from agreement to closing.

Wills and Estates

We assist individuals and families with wills, powers of attorney, and estate administration. Our goal is to provide clear advice and thoughtful planning so clients can make confident decisions about their future and their families. We guide clients through each step with care and attention to detail.

About

Amberlee Small

Amber is a lawyer practicing in Muskoka with a focus on real estate and wills and estates. She began her legal career in Australia in 2007.

Amber advises individuals and families on residential and recreational property transactions, as well as estate planning and estate administration. Her real estate practice reflects the unique character of Muskoka properties, including waterfront and rural matters where local experience can make a meaningful difference.

Many of the matters she handles involve significant personal or financial transitions. Amber focuses on clear advice, careful execution, and helping clients understand their options so they can move forward with confidence.

Based in Muskoka, Amber values strong working relationships with clients, realtors, and other professionals throughout the community. Her practice is supported by an experienced team, including Katherine, her law clerk, and Jenn, her office administrator, who help ensure files are managed efficiently and communication remains timely and consistent.
